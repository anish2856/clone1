Destinations

1. Taj Mahal, Agra

The Taj Mahal, an iconic white marble mausoleum, is one of the most famous landmarks in the world.

2. Jaipur, Rajasthan

Known as the "Pink City," Jaipur boasts stunning palaces, forts, and vibrant markets.

3. Goa

Goa is a popular beach destination with a relaxed atmosphere, beautiful coastlines, and a rich cultural heritage.

4. Kerala Backwaters

Explore the serene backwaters of Kerala by houseboat, surrounded by lush greenery and tranquil waters.

5. Varanasi, Uttar Pradesh

Varanasi is one of the oldest cities in the world and a spiritual hub along the banks of the Ganges River.

6. Leh-Ladakh, Jammu and Kashmir

Experience breathtaking landscapes, monasteries, and a unique culture in the Leh-Ladakh region.
